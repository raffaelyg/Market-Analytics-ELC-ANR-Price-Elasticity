# Behavioural Analytics & Growth Recovery: Prestige Beauty Index
## 📌 Project Overview
This repository details a strategic 31-day consulting project (Nov 2022 – Jan 2023) designed to diagnose growth stagnation for the market-leading prestige serum, **Advanced Night Repair (ANR)**, in Mainland China.

The project moved beyond traditional metrics to solve a core executive dilemma: **Why is our buyer base expanding while our value growth remains stagnant?.**

## 💡 The "One Question" (Behavioural Focus)
Traditional research showed high brand affinity, yet sales were slowing. We asked:

> **"What specific 'Moment of Struggle' triggers a loyal prestige buyer to adopt a mass-market competitor, and how is our own promotion strategy grooming this behaviour?"**

## 🧠 Strategic Insights: The "Say-Do" Gap
By analysing Kantar Worldpanel single-source data, we identified three invisible barriers to growth:

**The Traction Centre Shift**: Serum was historically the primary driver for buyer recruitment, but momentum slowed as "High-Efficacy Creams" became the new traction centre for upgrading mass buyers.

**The Habit Loop Tax**: Years of aggressive promotion periods "groomed" consumers to only buy during "stock-up" windows, leading to longer lead times between purchases and stagnant value growth.

**The Competitor "Job"**: Emerging mass brands (e.g., Proya and QuadHA) are not just cheaper; they are "hired" to provide "Affordable High Efficacy" or "Niche Functional Repair" (e.g., anti-sensitivity), capturing 35% of buyers who outflow from the prestige category

## Final Recommendations
* **Recentre on Efficacy**: Reposition ANR to tackle the "Anti-Ageing" niche to compete with high-efficacy mass rivals.
* **SKU Discipline**: Focus on the 50mL "Sweet Spot" to maximise revenue-per-buyer and reduce the "stockpiling" friction caused nby 100mL + packs.
* **Remove Friction**: Streamline the EMR/Digital channel to purhcase to reduce the"mental calories" requried for non-promotional buying.
